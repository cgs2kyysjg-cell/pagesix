Welcome to the Page 6 Party. This year, the gossip writes itself — because you write it. Submit your hottest, most scandalous, completely-fabricated-or-not-at-all headlines. The best ones will be read aloud, printed, and immortalized at the party.

How It Works

  1. Hit the yellow Submit button — top right or bottom of the page.
  2. Write a headline. The juicier, sillier, or more dramatic, the better.
  3. Add your byline so we know who broke the story — or stay anonymous if you want.
  4. Send to press. The best headlines get the spotlight on May 25.
