Welcome to the Page Six Party. This year, you are the news. Submit your headlines from the year — gossip, favorite memories, funny moments, standout stories, mishaps, glow-ups, wins, losses, and absolute nonsense all welcome. The best ones will be read aloud, printed, and immortalized at the party.

Takes 30 seconds · Submissions are private

How It Works

  1. Hit the yellow Submit button — top right or bottom of the page.
  2. Write a headline. A favorite memory, a funny moment, a piece of gossip, a year-defining story — anything from your year goes.
  3. Add your byline so we know who broke the story — or stay anonymous if you want.
  4. Send to press. The best headlines get the spotlight on May 25.
